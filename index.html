My journey has been shaped by personal health challenges that began in childhood. Over the years, I’ve learned that the path to wellness isn’t just about seeking answers in a doctor’s office, but about understanding the impact of our everyday choices and committing to smarter, more intentional decisions.

NBsquared is more than just a platform for tips and advice; it’s a space for real stories and meaningful insights. I believe that small changes can lead to great successes, and I am excited to share what I’ve learned along the way!

About.

Since I was young, I’ve faced a series of health challenges — behavioural disorders, breathing problems, digestive issues, and allergies — that turned everyday life into a constant battle. My parents and I spent countless hours in consultations and tests, searching for answers that often seemed out of reach. These challengers chipped away at my self-esteem, leaving me feeling vulnerable and frustrated.

Over time, I began to recognise patterns in my (re)actions and became increasingly aware of the hidden dangers in everyday products — from the cosmetics I used to the food I ate and even the fabrics I wore. This awareness motivated me to rethink my routine, minimise exposure to harmful substances, and, wherever possible, eliminate entirely. What a journey it’s been!

Although I might still suffer from some of these conditions, I’ve learned how to better handle them and avoid triggers. After years of trial and error, I’ve finally experienced meaningful improvements — a true testament to the power of persistence and intentional lifestyle changes.

My purpose is to keep learning and refining my habits while sharing my journey with you, so together we can build healthier, happier lives — one box at a time.
