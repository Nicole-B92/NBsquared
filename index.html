About

Since childhood, I’ve struggled with ongoing allergies and skin challenges that, for many years, affected my self-esteem (and overall health) and set me on a relentless search for answers.

Over time, after consulting various specialists, studying extensively, and trying countless "solutions", I became increasingly aware of the hidden toxins lurking in the products we use every day and their impact on our health and wellbeing (from cosmetics and personal hygiene items, to cleaning products, kitchen utensils, foods, beverages and even the clothes we wear!). This awareness inspired me to rethink my habits and reduce my exposure to harmful substances.

While some conditions persist, I’ve learned how to manage them through intentional lifestyle shifts. My journey is proof that small, consistent steps grounded in science and (self-)awareness are the foundation of lasting wellbeing.

Now, I share my story and the lessons I’ve learned along the way to inspire and support others on their own journey towards a healthier, happier life in alignment with their purpose and values.
