Hello!
It's me, Nicole B.

I am a global citizen, entrepreneur, and International Life & Health Coach in training with Health Coaches Academy (HCA), bringing a wealth of multicultural experience to my work.

I help men and women who feel drained, disconnected, or stuck restore their energy, confidence, and wellbeing without falling for toxic trends or quick fixes that don’t work.

Through my 1:1 coaching programme, I combine evidence-based health science with (self-)awareness to create realistic, lasting change. My approach goes beyond nutrition and exercise to address the mind, body, and soul, with a strong focus on reducing exposure to harmful ingredients and avoiding extremes.

Having overcome my own health challenges through intentional lifestyle shifts, I bring both expertise and lived experience to deliver a warm, honest, results-driven coaching experience that empowers clients to achieve lasting, positive change.

About

Since childhood, I’ve faced ongoing health challenges that affected my self-esteem and led to years of searching for answers.

Over time, I became more aware of hidden toxins in everyday products, from the cosmetics we use to the food we eat and even the fabrics we wear, which inspired me to change my habits and reduce exposure to harmful substances.

While some conditions persist, I’ve learned how to manage them through intentional lifestyle shifts. My journey is proof that small, consistent actions, supported by awareness and community, can lead to lasting wellbeing.

Now, I share my story and knowledge to inspire and support others on their journey to a healthier, happier life.

Purpose

To inspire and empower you to live a healthier, happier life

by helping you reconnect with yourself and your environment, so you feel amazing in your own skin (pun intended).

Vision

To inspire a global shift towards conscious, toxin-aware living, making lifelong wellbeing accessible to all.
