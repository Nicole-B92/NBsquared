About

Since childhood, I have lived with ongoing allergies and skin challenges that, for many years, affected my self-esteem and overall health. This experience led me on a persistent search for answers.

Over time, through working with different specialists, studying extensively, and experimenting with countless approaches, I became increasingly aware of the hidden toxins present in everyday products and the impact they can have on our health and wellbeing.

From cosmetics and personal care to cleaning products, kitchen utensils, food, beverages, and even the clothes we wear, these exposures are often overlooked yet deeply influential. This awareness prompted me to rethink my habits and intentionally reduce my exposure to harmful substances.

While some conditions remain part of my life, I have learned how to manage them through conscious, science-informed lifestyle shifts. My journey has shown me that lasting wellbeing is not built through extremes, but through small, consistent actions rooted in awareness and self-respect.

Today, I share my story and the lessons I have learned to support others in navigating their own path towards a healthier, more balanced life, one that feels aligned with their values, identity, and purpose.
