Hello!
It's me, Nicole B.

I am an Entrepreneur and an International Life & Health Coach in training. My purpose is to inspire and empower you to live a healthier, happier life — through knowledge of yourself and your environment, so you feel great in your own skin (pun intended)!

I created NBsquared to promote self-awareness and educate you on how our habits and the hidden toxins in everyday products affect our physical and mental health, empowering you to make more informed, confident choices that contribute to your overall wellbeing.

You can find me on social media, where I share tips and insights based on both my personal experience and research. Through storytelling, I offer glimpses of my daily life and the knowledge I have gained over the years to support you on your journey.

About

Since I was young, due to a series of health challenges, my parents and I spent countless hours in medical consultations and tests, searching for answers that often felt just out of reach. These experiences gradually wore down my self-esteem, leaving me feeling vulnerable and frustrated.

Over time, I started paying closer attention to my mind and body, becoming increasingly aware of the hidden dangers in everyday products — from the cosmetics I used to the food I ate and even the fabrics I wore. This awareness motivated me to rethink my routines, and to minimise exposure to harmful substances. What a transformative journey it’s been!

Although I still face some of the conditions from my childhood, I’ve learned how to manage them more effectively and avoid potential triggers. After years of trial and error, I’ve achieved meaningful improvements — a true testament to the power of persistence and intentional lifestyle changes.

I truly believe in the power of small, consistent actions and the importance of a strong support system. My goal is to lead by example, sharing my journey and insights with you while continuing to study and refine my habits. Together, we can build healthier, happier lives — one step at a time.

Shop
